About Our District

The church started in 1986 as House Fellowship in a shop at Ago Giwa along Railway line, Ogba-yo; as a result of the vision of the District Overseer (Rev.E. O. Oluwagbesan).

It was later moved to a rented land at Ago-Igunnu(Ago Jesu) at one Mr Folarin's residence.

The church secured a plot of land behind the dispensary which later become controversial and was never recovered. The church later secured two and half plots of land at Oremeji Street, off Agoro road, ijoko in 1983 where the church is presently located.

Our Mission

We exist to honour God, proclaim the gospel, and demonstrate Christ’s love through service and discipleship in the Ijoko area and beyond.

Our Vision

Healthy churches multiplying disciples who impact families and communities for Christ.
